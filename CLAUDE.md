Spinner entries should primarily be **verbs** in present participle form (e.g., "Deploying to prod", "Brewing a fresh pot", "Questioning my career choices"). They should read naturally after a spinner symbol like `⠋ Deploying to prod...`. Rare exceptions for iconic character catchphrases are okay (e.g., "Savvy?"), but the vast majority of entries should be verb phrases.

When adding a new spinner pack, also add it to the table in `skills/install-spinner/SKILL.md` (pack name, short description, and a couple of example verbs).
